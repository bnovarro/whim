Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Whim ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account information — your name, email address, and profile photo when you sign up.
  • Location — your approximate location while using the app, to find nearby venues and plans. We do not track location in the background.
  • Activity data — the plans (Whims) you create, join, or view inside the app.
  • Device token — your push notification token, so we can send you reminders about your plans.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show you nearby venues and spontaneous plans
  • To let friends find and join your Whims
  • To send you relevant push notifications and reminders
  • To improve the app experience

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not track your location when the app is closed
  • We do not share your information with advertisers

Third-Party Services

Whim uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Google — optional sign-in with Google
  • Expo — push notification delivery

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Whim is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

  • Access or update your profile information in the app at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and data by emailing us
  • Opt out of push notifications in your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll post the updated version here with a new date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
