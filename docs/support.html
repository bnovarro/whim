Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a Whim?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen. You can describe what you're in the mood for, pick a time, and Whim will suggest nearby spots. Once you're happy, tap Launch to send it to your friends.

Why does Whim need my location?

Location is used to find venues and plans near you. We only access it while you're using the app — never in the background.

I'm not receiving notifications. What do I do?

Go to your iPhone Settings → Whim → Notifications and make sure notifications are enabled. Also check that you haven't muted the app inside Whim's notification settings.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at brandonnovarro@gmail.com and we'll delete your account and all associated data within 7 days.

Can I change my profile photo or name?

Yes — tap your profile icon in the app to edit your name and photo at any time.

The app crashed or something isn't working.

Try closing and reopening the app first. If the issue persists, send us an email describing what happened and we'll look into it quickly.
